House Music and EDM DJ

Klarius is 22 years old, born in South Italy and he has been living in Malta for the past 5 years. He had the passion for music since he was little, about 8 years old, but never had enough time and especially enough money to deepen such passion. He has been practicing and performing all of his favorite tracks on his laptop since he was 12 (the time when he earned enough money by doing small jobs so he could finally buy himself a laptop) and never had the opportunity to touch and feel a proper Dj console before. He focuses himself on house music and EDM; around 2 years ago he decided to step up and fight for the biggest dream of his life, becoming a professional DJ. In the summer of 2016, he managed to be picked out to start a Dj Academy in Malta, where he had the chance to pass all his knowledge he had learnt by using his laptop into a professional Dj console. Such academy lasted 3 months and afterwards he finally started playing in local clubs. He has been known under the name of DJ Bruni for years, until 2019, when, through a rebranding initiative, he is now known as Klarius, a name that truly reflects his upcoming music as a DJ.

In addition to that, he decided to step up even more by giving himself a huge opportunity to improve his music knowledge and skills with the number one Maltese Dj in Malta, Joseph Armani. He started a Dj course that lasted four months with him where he learnt new skills and new tricks and also learned new aspects and characteristics about music he didn’t even know they existed. Now Klarius is the founder of a new organization/event planner brought up by local Djs called Projekt Noir, which will help him and other Djs to perform all over the Maltese clubs. Klarius’ dream is making music and becoming a professional Dj in all the aspects and he is already working very hard to achieve such goal. To further his career he is going to start the path of creating his own music from February, 2019, when he is going to overtake a course in music production.

Klarius’ tagline: Hear the music. Feel the beat. At times, I fit into music more than I fit into life because the secret is to listen to more music and less advice.
